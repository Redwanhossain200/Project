Steps to Milk a Cow

  1. Approach the cow calmly and gently.
  2. Wash your hands thoroughly.
  3. Clean the cow's udder with a damp cloth.
  4. Position a clean bucket or container under the udder.
  5. Gently squeeze and pull the teats to release milk.
  6. Continue milking until the udder is empty.
  7. Remove the bucket and secure the udder with a clean cloth.
  8. Store the milk in a clean, covered container.
